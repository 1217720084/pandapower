##########################
About pandapower
##########################

pandapower combines the data analysis library `pandas <http://pandas.pydata.org/>`_ and the power flow solver `PYPOWER <https://pypi.python.org/pypi/PYPOWER>`_ to create an easy to use network calculation program
aimed at automation of power system analysis and optimization in distribution and sub-transmission networks.

pandapower is a joint development of the Department for Distribution System Operation, University of Kassel and the Department for Distribution System Operation at the Fraunhofer Institute for Wind Energy and Energy System
Technology (IWES), Kassel.



It is released under a BSD-style license.
